About Bulb

Early people used candles and oil lamps for light.
Humphry Davy invented the electric arclight in the early 1800s.
Thomas Alva Edison, Joseph Swann, and several others in the 1880s invented the more efficient incandescent lightbulb.

Types of Bulb:

  • Fluorescent.

  • Halogen.

  • Mercury Vapor Lamps.
